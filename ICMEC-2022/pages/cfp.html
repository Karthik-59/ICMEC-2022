Call for paper

The Conference theme is “Modern Electronic Components” Technical papers describing original, previously unpublished research, not currently under review by another conference or journal, are lies in the current theme of the conference can be submitted under following tracks: Paper submissions are invited in all topics related to the computer aided developments of the Internet of things typically the following but not limited to:

Track 1: Electronic devices

  • Modelling and testing
  • CAD tool and fabrication technology
  • Power devices
  • Sensors

Track 2: Communication systems and components

  • RF and optical components
  • Millimetre-wave technology
  • 5G gears
  • System architecture and protocols

Track 3: AI for electronic components and Systems

  • Signal synthesis and processing
  • Internet of things(IOT)
  • Computer network and security
  • Wireless sensor network

Submission guidelines

Full papers must be original and novel research contributions with evaluation results and are limited to six (5) printed pages (10-point font) including figures, references and appendices (two additional pages are allowed with a per-page extra fee). Short papers, limited in length to four (4) pages, should be more visionary in nature and are meant to discuss new challenges and visions, showcase early research results, and explore novel research directions. All the manuscript should be submitted using easy chair submission link in PDF format without author name and affiliation as per the Springer format (See Download).

Authors should ensure that the similarity score of their research paper is not above 10 %. Manuscripts having similarity score more than 10 % shall not be processed.

Review Process and Oral Representation

  • The paper having the similarity score less than 10 % shall be processed for double blind review.
  • The papers will be peer reviewed by technical committee of the conference.
  • The papers will be peer reviewed by technical committee of the conference:

    • (a) Oral presentation for accepted paper
    (b) Accepted with minor corrections for Oral Presentation
    (c) Rejected.
